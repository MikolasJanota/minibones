minibones is a tool for computing a backbone of a given CNF based on the articles [1,2].

The option -i is recommended for all except the default mode. So for instance, one would use 
minibone -i -e -c 100

--Mikolas
May 2013

[1] On Computing Backbones of Propositional Theories
Joao Marques-Silva, Mikolas Janota, and Inês Lynce
The 19th European Conference on Artificial Intelligence (ECAI '10).

[2] Algorithms for Computing Backbones of Propositional Formulae
Mikolas Janota, Inês Lynce, and Joao Marques-Silva
RCRA 2012 AI-Com special issue
